#  PulseNet — AI-Powered Medical Diagnosis & Clinical Decision Support System

PulseNet is an *AI-powered medical diagnosis and clinical decision support platform* designed to assist doctors and healthcare professionals in analyzing patient symptoms and vital signs to generate *probable diagnoses, confidence scores, treatment guidance, and referral recommendations*.

The system is especially tailored for *primary healthcare centers, rural clinics, and low-resource medical environments*, where instant access to specialists may not be available.

## Overview

PulseNet bridges the gap between *clinical expertise and AI intelligence* by offering:
- AI-driven symptom analysis
- Risk-based specialist referral recommendations
- Clear, explainable diagnostic insights
- Persistent patient diagnosis records
- Multilingual health query support

It is *not a replacement for doctors, but a **clinical assistant* that enhances decision-making speed, accuracy, and consistency.

---

## Problem Statement

In many healthcare settings:
- Doctors handle *high patient volume*
- Access to specialists is *limited*
- Diagnosis is often based on *manual symptom correlation*
- Rural clinics lack *advanced diagnostic tools*

These challenges can lead to:
- Delayed diagnosis
- Missed severity indicators
- Increased workload for clinicians

---

## Solution Overview

PulseNet provides:
- A *secure doctor login system*
- An *AI Diagnostic Suite* for structured patient input
- An *AI Inference Engine* for intelligent analysis
- A *Diagnosis Output Module* with clear next steps
- A *Multilingual Health Chatbot* for patient education
- A *Patient Records System* for follow-up and audit

## Key Features

### Doctor Authentication
- Secure login for registered doctors
- Role-based access control
- Personalized dashboard

### AI Diagnostic Suite
Doctors can enter:
- Patient demographics (Age, Gender)
- Vital signs (Temperature, BP, Pulse, Weight)
- Symptoms and clinical notes

One-click *“Generate AI Diagnosis”* produces structured results.

### AI Inference Engine
The AI engine performs:
- Symptom correlation
- Severity assessment
- Differential diagnosis generation
- Confidence score calculation
- Medical sanity checks (e.g., invalid temperature units)
- Treatment & isolation recommendations

### Specialist Referral Detection
Automatically flags high-risk cases and advises *immediate specialist consultation*.

### Multilingual Health Chatbot
- Answers health-related questions
- Supports multiple Indian languages
- Improves accessibility for patients and caregivers

### Patient Records Management
- Stores diagnosis history
- Enables doctors to review past consultations
- Helps in follow-up and continuity of care

## System Architecture

*High-level flow:*

1. Doctor logs into the system
2. Doctor accesses AI Diagnostic Suite
3. Patient data is submitted
4. AI Inference Engine analyzes inputs
5. Diagnosis & recommendations are generated
6. Results are stored and displayed
7. Chatbot assists with additional queries

---

## AI Diagnostic Workflow

1. *Input Validation*
   - Checks physiological plausibility (e.g., temperature units)

2. *Symptom Pattern Matching*
   - Matches symptoms against known clinical patterns

3. *Severity Assessment*
   - Identifies red-flag indicators

4. *Differential Diagnosis*
   - Generates possible conditions with explanations

5. *Confidence Scoring*
   - Provides likelihood estimation for diagnosis

6. *Clinical Advice Generation*
   - Suggests tests, medications, isolation, or referrals
